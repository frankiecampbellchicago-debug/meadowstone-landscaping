Our Story

Craftsmanship You'll See Every Time You Pull Into Your Driveway

MeadowStone was founded on a straightforward belief: a well-kept property isn't a luxury — it's an investment, a source of daily pleasure, and a reflection of the care you put into your home.

We work exclusively in the Chicagoland suburbs, which means we understand this specific climate — the late spring frosts, the hot July dry spells, the brutal freeze-thaw cycles that destroy lesser hardscaping. That local knowledge is baked into everything we recommend and everything we build.

Every MeadowStone project is overseen by a senior landscape manager, not handed off to a rotating crew. When you call, someone who knows your property answers.
